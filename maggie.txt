Maggie Ingredients
==================
1) Maggie Noddles
2) Onions
3) Tomatoes
4) Water
5) Oil
6) Spring onions
7) chile oil

Procedure:
1) Chop onions and tomatoes
2) Put oil in the pan and saute the onions until they are golden brown
3) Boil nooddles as per packet instructions and keep it aside...keep some of the water to use as a thickener.
